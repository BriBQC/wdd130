Site Name

Whitewater Rafting Adventures

Site Purpose

We provide a service for users to go on whitewater rafting adventures. This includes information on the basics of whitewater rafting, the ability to sign up for a guided adventure at beginner to advanced levels, prices, trip duration, and availability.

Site Audience

We cater to families, individuals, and groups looking for a rafting experience. Participants must be ages 10 and up. The health of the individual should be considered by consulting a physician before signing up. Our site answers these questions:

  • Where can I go whitewater rafting with this company?
  • How much does it cost and what is included with my adventure?
  • What do I need to bring on my rafting trip? What do I wear?
  • How long does the trip last?
  • Who are the guides and what is their experience?
