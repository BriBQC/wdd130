Yoga for the MIND

Yoga has been proven to bring a myriad of mental health benefits. From better focus and concentration, to renewed purpose and retention. The combination of movement with breath allows maximum oxygen to reach the brain and very ends to each limb. Most yoga practitioners find this effect to bring a clarity and sharpness to their minds and have reported imporoved job functioning, relationships, retention of information, and general drive to be. Our Hatha and Pranayama classes will help you to feel these benefits for yourselves. A class is waiting for you, come join us!

Yoga for the BODY

Yoga is so much more than a good stretch! Here at Breathe Yoga you'll find classes that tone your muscles, get your heart rate up, strengthen your core, relieve pain, improve your balance, flexibility, digestion, circulation, and body awareness. Yoga is good for your health all around and is recommended by health experts around the world. If you're interested in a workout that works up a sweat, check out a vinyasa or warrior class. Be challenged physcially and mentally in these practices.

Yoga for the SPIRIT

Our lives get hectic and stressful quickly, we lose connection to ourselves, others, and our higher power. Yoga provides a wonderful way to relax and reconnect. In all our classes you'll find greater connections and peace especially as you develop a regular practice. The more you work on breath body connections the deeper your spiritual connections will be. Our yin and yoga nidra classes are the best places to start finding your higher power, plugging in, and bringing a sense of peace and purpose back to your life.
