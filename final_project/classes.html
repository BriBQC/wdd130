Hatha Yoga

Beginner | 60 minutes | Monday, Wednesday, & Saturday 6am

Slow pacing, careful breakdowns of poses and variations, breathwork, and a good full body stretch make this the perfect class for beginners. Newcomers to yoga will love the slower pace and detailed instructions as well as learning the basic flow of a class from intentions to shavasana. This class is great if you need to focus your mind, get deeper understanding of the poses, or just need a good stretch without a cardio workout, this is the class for you. Benefits include full body stretch, deeper pose breakdowns, breathwork, clarity of mind, and refreshed muscles.

Pranayama Yoga

Beginner to Advanced | 20 minutes | Free Online Sessions

Our pranayama classes are all about breathwork. We only assume a small handful of postures in these classes. If you are new to pranayama we recommend staring with our pranayama basics video which will go over the postures, mudras, and expectations of the classes. We have beginner, intermediate, and advanced versions of each pranayama class. Each excercise will guide you through a pranayama technique step by step, then an actual practice with the instructor, and finally a reflection moment where we encourage you to ponder the sensations and thoughts the practice brought to you. Each practice lists the benefits ranging from calming to energizing, mental clarity to improved circulation. Free to all members, we encourage working through these videos until you are comfortable with the techniques taught. In our advanced classes we use pranayama flows to warm up and help cool down.

Vinyasa Yoga

Beginner to Advanced | 60 minutes | Tuesday & Thursday 6am

Vinyasa is the next level from our Hatha yoga classes. You'll find the same poses and breathwork as introduced in hatha, but at a faster pace. Here you will practice connecting the breath to movement. As you breathe in you change poses, as you breathe out you change again, and so on as instructed by the teacher. In vinyasa you'll build up a comfortable level of heat and might even find yourself sweating! Perfect for toning and mild cardio needs as well as mental focus, improved balance, clearer thoughts and deeper connection to breath.

Warrior Yoga

Intermediate to Advanced | 90 minutes | Friday & Saturday 9am

Need to feel strong, empowered, or awaken the fire within? Warrior is the class for you! Building on basic poses and adding the next level of intensity we flow fast and we flow strong in this practice. If you are looking to learn or step up your strength in poses this class will help you build a strong core and level you up variation by variation to that beautiful arm balance or chatarunga that you've been dreaming of. This class offers more challenging poses and will help you build you up to them as you listen to your body and build on it's promptings. This class gets your heart pumping and your muscles burning. Come ready to work hard!

Yin Yoga

Beginner to Advanced | 60 minutes | Tuesday & Thursday 7pm

This practice is all about the deep muscles and connective tissues and meditation. In yin we have a gentle warm up to get the energy flowing and then we move into the yin postures. Yin requires us to move into a posture just until we feel the stretch and then we hold the posture, breath full and deep, and feel our body slowly melt into the stretch. You'll find in yin that you'll be able to move deeper into a posture than you can in a regular yoga class. Yin classes will leave you with a deep sense of relaxation and peace. Perfect for those who need to work out deeper tension, improve the stretch of muscles, or need full body relaxation will find it in this class. Ending with a long shavasana to integrate all the work done in these deepening postures you're sure to leave feeling euphoric.

Yoga Nidra

Beginner to Advanced | 60 minutes | Wednesday 7pm

The practice of yoga nidra is translated as "yogic sleep". Yes, we are in a laying down posture the entire time, and yes we look like we are sleeping. However, this class is anything but sleeping. Yoga nidra is about attaining that space between wake and sleep where your subconscious reigns supreme. Our instructors will start by guiding you into the best nidra position for your body. Then they will proceed to instruct you on relaxing each and every individual part of your body, one at a time. The process is slow precisely so you can work on any difficult spots and attain as deep a state of subconsciousness as possible. Soft music, dim lighting, a calm soothing voice walking you through the steps all help to facilitate this yogic sleep. Benefits include clarity of mind and spirit, connection to our higher selves, deep relaxation for mind and body, and a sense of overall peace.
