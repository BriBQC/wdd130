Site Name

Breathe Yoga Studio

Site Purpose

To provide information about Breathe Yoga Studio. A page about our studio including location, hours, and contact information. A page about our instructors and their certifications/qualifications. A page where interested clents can find class dates, times, sign-up sheets, and links/info on about our virtual classes. A page dedicated to teaching clients about the yoga styles that are taught in our classes, common sanskrit phrases they will hear at our studio, and the meaning and significance of mantras. We also include a page for our clients to shop online at our Breathe Store. And finally, a page that answers frequently asked questions.

Site Audience

Breathe Yoga Studio is for beginner to advanced level yogis. We offer group and private classes for adults (18+) throughout the week (Mon-Sat). All participants are required to sign a waiver and are encouraged to consult their doctor before attending a yoga class. We offer classes at affordable prices at the group level, prices increase at the private level based on length and target need of client.

Our site answers these questions:

  • Where is your studio located? What are your hours?
  • How much does it cost for a group class? Private class?
  • What do I need to bring to a yoga class? What do I wear?
  • Where can I buy the yoga mat I saw the instructor using?
  • What is pranayama yoga? What is hatha yoga?
  • What certifications do your instructors have?
  • Do you offer virtual classses?

Home Page Content

Mind

Looking for more clarity, better focus, and positive thoughts? Our Hatha yoga classes with their calm, focused flows bring mindfulness to the practice and leaves with you a sense of focus, clarity, and brightness. Or try one of our Pranayama sessions that combine a new breath technique each week with an easy, meditative flow to help bring oxygen to the brain and limbs.

Include a "Learn more" button that takes them to the page about yoga styles and the Hatha section. Another to learn more about Pranayama sessions. And a third button to sign up for a class.

Body

Who says yoga can't make you sweat! Our Vinyasa classes and Warrior sessions bring on the heat with more challenges poses, longer holds, and a faster pace. Vinyasa will get you moving with a full body cardio flow workout. Our warrior sessions focus on building strength.

Include a "Learn more" button that takes them to the page about yoga styles and the Vinyasa section. Another to learn more about Warrior sessions. And a third button to sign up for a class.

Spirit

Yoga has the power to connect us to ourselves, others, and our higher power. All of our classes include the option to use mantras and intentions during flows, and final shavasana as a way to connect. However, our restorative Yin classes and Yoga Nidra sessions are planned to help focus on connections, letting go of what no longer serves us, and finding peace.

Include a "Learn more" button that takes them to the page about yoga styles and the Yin section. Another to learn more about Yoga Nidra sessions. And a third button to sign up for a class.
