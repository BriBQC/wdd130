Site Name

Prana Yoga Studio

Site Purpose

To provide information about Prana Yoga Studio. A page about our studio including location, hours, and contact information. A page about our instructors and their certifications/qualifications. A page where interested clents can find class dates, times, sign-up sheets, and links/info on about our virtual classes. A page dedicated to teaching clients about the yoga styles that are taught in our classes, common sanskrit phrases they will hear at our studio, and the meaning and significance of mantras. We also include a page for our clients to shop online at our Prana Store. And finally, a page that answers frequently asked questions.

Site Audience

Prana Yoga Studio is for beginner to advanced level yogis. We offer group and private classes for adults (18+) throughout the week (Mon-Sat). All participants are required to sign a waiver and are encouraged to consult their doctor before attending a yoga class. We offer classes at affordable prices at the group level, prices increase at the private level based on length and target need of client.

Our site answers these questions:

  • Where is your studio located? What are your hours?
  • How much does it cost for a group class? Private class?
  • What do I need to bring to a yoga class? What do I wear?
  • Where can I buy the yoga mat I saw the instructor using?
  • What is pranayama yoga? What is hatha yoga?
  • What certifications do your instructors have?
  • Do you offer virtual classses?
