Plan Your Adventure

Scenic Trips

Day Trip

A scenic day trip is the perfect adventure for anyone who is new to rafting, looking for some beautiful nature photos, or a fun day activity for the family. Departing from our very own dock on the Snake River, you'll enjoy a calm, trip down the river. Some of the highlights include the Whisper Falls, Layered Cake Wall, and Blue Lagoon. Ending at McCurdie Wash where a bus will be waiting to take you back to base. With plenty of time for photos, and lots of local info from our guides, this trip is a crowd pleaser.

Day trips depart at 10:00 AM and return at 3:00 PM.

Lunch and snacks are provided.

Overnight Trip

Our scenic overnight trips are the perfect union of a scenic river experience and a good old family camping trip. Enjoy the day on the river and catch all the highlights of our regular scenic day trip. Then we pull into Hardy's Bend to camp overnight. Wake up to fresh mountain air and enjoy a warm breakfast before we hop in the rafts for a quick float to see the Ribbon Winder and The View. We land at Johnson Stretch where buses will take you back to base.

Overnight Trips depart on Fridays at 10:00 AM and return on Saturday at 11:00 AM.

Lunch, dinner, breakfast, and snacks are provided.

Whitewater Trips

Day Trip

A day trip is perfect for whitewater beginners. Prepare to move fast and get wet. This day trip is nicely spaced with calms between the rapids for adjustments and instructions from the guide. We start at McCurdie Wash and end at Old Mill. Highlights of this trip include the Ribbon Winder, Lucky Duck, and Widow Maker. It's equal parts fun, excitement, and splashes! A short bus trip to the take off point and a bus trip back to base are part of the trip.

Day trips depart at 10:00 AM and return at 4:00 PM.

Lunch and snacks provided.

Overnight Trip

What's better than one day of whitewater mania? Two days!! With our completely planned-for-you overnight trips, all you need to worry about is what you're going to wear. It better be water proof, because you will get wet! We start at McCurdie Wash and end at Survivor's Point. Highlights of this trip include Grinder, Bernadine's Blister, Gut Check, Wipeout, The Big One, and many many more. Camp over night at Gulver's Wake and get up and go again in the morning!

Overnight trips depart on Fridays at 10:00 AM and return on Saturdays at 2:00 PM.

Two lunches, dinner, breakfast, and snacks are provided.
