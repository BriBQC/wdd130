Meet Our Team

Owners and Operators

Zack and Gloria Waint have been running rivers together for 15 years. They even met on at a river guide training! They opened Whitewater Rafting together because they want to share their love and expertize with you. Zach first learned to tame the whitewater when he was 12 years old. His father taught him everything he knows. Gloria took her first trip on the river during a spring break from her college studies of biology. She was hooked! Whitewater Rafting is their baby and they put all their love into it. They can't wait to see you out there!

Meet Alex! Not only does Alex know the river like the back of his hand, but he is also a professional photographer. When you get your beautiful river adventure photos after a trip with us, you can thank him! In the off season, Alex runs his own photography studio and enjoys skiing, blogging, and catching up on his favorite shows.

Jenn lives and breathes on the water. When she isn't guiding one of our adventures she is out there kayaking, sailing, swimming, and even river ice skating. Originally from Alaska, Jenn has been with our team for 5 years now. She knows all the best spots for a good view or a good splash.

Give a big welcome to Mark, the newest member of our team. Mark is currently studying for a degree in environmental science. When he isn't studying or guiding our rafts through the drops and stretches, he is busy making the world a better place. He enjoys volunteering, recycling, tutoring, and fly fishing.

If it weren't Angie, none of this would work! Angie takes care of everything in the office. From answering phones, keeping the calendar, paying the bills, and updating the website, Angie has it covered. Angie is an animal lover and owns three dogs, five cats, two goats, a turtle, and a salt water tank full of beautiful fish.

Celeste is the heart and sole of adventure. She makes it all happen. When you book a trip with us, Celeste is the one you will be hearing from to plan out all the details. Got a question about your trip? Call Celeste! She'll make sure you are comfortable, confident, and excited before you even arrive at the dock. Her other hobbies include rock climbing, archery, and cooking.
